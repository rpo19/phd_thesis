This chapter presents the contributions for \ref{ob:extraction_assessment}---through an assessment of existing \ac{ee} techniques applied to the legal domain.
First, in \Cref{sec:ijckg}, we address the \emph{novel entity challenge}---an important problem for the legal domain, since court and investigative documents often mention persons that are not known in public \acl{kr}, but whose recognition and linking may enable tasks like precedent case retrieval or interlinking heterogeneous documents mentionin suspects during an investigation.

Secondly we study to which extent general-domain \ac{nlp} appoaches for \acl{ee} work on the Italian legal domain.
In \Cref{sec:eejudgements} focusing on Italian judgments from civil courts and in \Cref{sec:eechats} chat logs from an Italian investigation.
